What's Registered Intellectual Property?

"Registered Intellectual Property (RIP) is a Blockchain based distribution and consumption platform for music. It's built for the artists and consumers of this content."

"Artists are you tired of the middle man always taking 'their' cut? Well RIP gives you the power and opportunity to cut out the corporate middle men such as record labels, publishers, etc. allowing you to publish your work fast and independently"

"As a customer you already know what you like, or maybe not? With RIP you can easily find you favorite songs and/or artists, or discover something or someone entirely new."


What is the "The Blockchain"?

“The Blockchain is a tool we can use to make a secure peer to peer transaction. It’s called the block chain because it is made out of ‘blocks’. These blocks simply reflect transactions that are being made. Every 10 minutes or so the new blocks are validated to ensure the trade was made and none of the information was tampered with. More blocks mean a more secure chain.”

“We ensure this security throw the use of trusted and established blockchains such as Ethereum™. Every time a transaction is to be made a smart contract is made with the Ethereum™ to represent the purchase transaction, after both parties have confirmed the transaction is made and it is added to a block in the chain. The consumer gets the agreed on music added to their library, and the artist receives their commission”
